Spatial audio simulation system.

- Collaborated with a multi-disciplinary team to deliver audio support for the PRC 70th Anniversary Parade simulation system.
- Architected and implemented the sound engine integration between Wwise and Unreal Engine 4, binding ambient, effects, and voice-over assets to 3D parade formations.
- Recorded, designed, mixed, and rendered 5.1 surround-sound sequences for the final pre-visualization reel.
- Deployed and calibrated on-site review audio rig for iterative optimization of spatial audio cues.
